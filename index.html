Pension in Australia: What You Need to Know

The pension system in Australia provides financial assistance to retirees, ensuring they can maintain a decent standard of living. Understanding the different types of pensions available and their eligibility criteria is essential for planning your retirement.

Types of Pension in Australia

  • Age Pension – A government-funded pension for eligible retirees based on income and assets tests.
  • Superannuation – A retirement savings scheme where employers contribute to an individual's fund throughout their working life.
  • Disability Support Pension – Financial assistance for individuals who cannot work due to a disability.
  • Veteran Pensions – Special pensions provided to retired military personnel.
